Tsinghua University - Information Art & Design (AI Track)

Beijing, China 2-3 years ¥6-25万 total Highly Competitive

Tsinghua University's Academy of Arts & Design offers a cutting-edge program at the intersection of information technology and artistic practice, with specialized tracks in AI applications for art and design.

Program Highlights

  • Focus on AI applications in visual arts, interactive installations, and design innovation
  • Access to Tsinghua's world-class engineering and computer science resources
  • Strong emphasis on Chinese cultural context in digital art creation
  • Opportunities for industry collaboration with leading tech companies

Admission Requirements

  • Portfolio demonstrating artistic ability and technical skills
  • HSK 5 (210+) for Chinese-taught programs
  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Strong performance in national entrance exam for domestic students

Curriculum Focus

AI Art Theory Generative Design Interactive Systems Data Visualization VR/AR Art Creative Coding
